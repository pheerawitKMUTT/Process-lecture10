## 1. Executive Summary
### Production Capacity

## 2. Process Chemistry
### Reaction
- The process chemistry for the production of Sodium Hypochlorite is as follows:
  
  \[ 2 \text{NaOH} + \text{Cl}_2 \rightarrow \text{NaOCl} + \text{H}_2\text{O} + \text{NaCl} \]
